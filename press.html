Few Words About Us

Preferred Jewelers started with a teenager in 1978 wanting to build his own business. Larry Nichols wanted to provide people with his favorite thing in the world, diamonds. His saying was "dogs may be a guys best friend, but diamonds are everyones best friend." He was determined to sell diamonds to as many people as she could. He started off with cheaper products and worked his way up to getting other jewelry companies to invest their product to his so she could sell them at his jewelry store. His business was slow at first, but it quickly picked up pace and is now one of the top jewelry stores in the U.S.
